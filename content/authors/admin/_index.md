I received my BA (Hons.) from Simon Fraser University (in Vancouver, Canada), my PhD from Yale University, and my postdoctoral training from the University of Chicago Booth School of Business. Before graduate school, I worked as a research coordinator for two years at the Stanford Social Neuroscience Laboratory. I am a content expert in social, moral, and motivational psychology, and have a strong metholodoligical background in neuroimaging, computational modelling, and most recently: AI.
